WHO WE ARE

Established in 2012 by Barry Millar and Neil Adams, Verve Concepts tailored approach to construction positions our clients at the forefront of the business. Neil’s background managing complex high-end construction projects brings a wealth of knowledge, ensuring exacting standards of build quality and meticulous attention to detail are upheld. Combined with Barry’s experience working in financial markets and property development the team dynamic means every project is approached with precision and attention to detail, supported by rigorous internal working processes that deliver the smoothest possible client experience.

Our services are provided with complete transparency. Ultimately, we want to realise the very best build experience, implemented to the highest quality and delivered at the most competitive price.

Verve Concepts has completed projects in Belgravia, Chelsea, Mayfair, Knightsbridge, Kensington, and across some of the prime locations within Surrey and the South East including; Cobham, Esher, Oxshott and Weybridge. We invite you to explore our work and learn more about what we do.

OUR TEAM

Verve Concepts team comprises of skilled trades, project managers, an office-based support team as well as our carefully curated roster of suppliers. We value our dedicated team, nurturing their talent through training and CPD to ensure they are performing at the highest level.

A key factor in the success of each project is the co-ordination and engagement with our long- standing team of consultants. Our established network of industry recognised Interior Designers, Architects and Consultants makes for a streamlined and seamless process for our clients.

RELATIONSHIPS

With a management process that encompasses a flexible and adaptable approach to suit each client’s needs and fulfil individual project requirements we place clients front and centre of our business model. Our clients trust us to deliver their projects because our management process values transparency and allows for control over their investment. Clear client communication is of the utmost importance to us and we take great care and pride in realising our client’s vision.

We value our longstanding client relationships and as such we undertake a strict policy of client discretion to ensure our clients privacy is maintained throughout.

QUALITY

We are committed to quality; every detail is accounted for as we understand the great care and attention to detail that clients, architects and designers put into their enterprise. Our core work is high-end luxury residential projects and the team have a wealth of experience in achieving the premium finishes expected. This ensures that quality and design details are checked continuously throughout the project to exceed client expectations at the final handover.
