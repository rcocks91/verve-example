Bespoke Residential Development

Verve Concepts is a high-end residential construction company based in London and Surrey specialising in luxury residential properties for discerning private clients.

From the creation of bespoke new build homes to luxury residential refurbishments Verve Concepts highly professional team is dedicated to delivering the finest quality of workmanship in exquisite private residences across Surrey and London

WHO ARE WE AT VERVE?

Founded by Barry Millar and Neil Adams in 2012 Verve Concepts has steadily gained a reputation for delivering outstanding build quality and a highly personalised service in London and Surrey’s most luxurious homes. Our dedicated project teams, as well as our skilled on-site staff provide a comprehensive service to deliver projects to our exacting standards

about us

We are committed to providing exceptional client care throughout each project and our collaborative approach ensures projects are managed efficiently with meticulous attention to quality and detail.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Specialists in the refurbishment and construction of premium residential properties across London and Surrey, Verve Concepts undertake build projects from complex restorations and award-winning contemporary architectural homes through to luxurious new build homes.
